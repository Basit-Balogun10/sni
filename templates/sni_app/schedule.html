Join the Discussion - Schedule

After reserving a spot on our booking page, the mentee will receive an email containing the details of the session booked for, the details include the zoom meeting link. Mentee comes in and meets with the mentor to walk them through the 'Synergising' period, after which the mentee will be asked to give more detail on the area where help is needed. The mentor will then decide on a pathway forward, and the mentorship begins for a period decided by the mentor based on the mentee's needs.

How to run an Online-based Organization or initiative?

  • Apps & website to be used when running online operations
  • Methods of collaborating with teams virtually
  • Process chart & work-flow creation (online versions)
  • Creating an organizational culture that supersedes ‘artificial-ness’ & formalities of online communication
  • How to attract your target audience to an online event?
  • How to foster individual accountability & keep motivation at a high?
